Problem

Small finance teams reconcile via spreadsheets, causing 3–5 day lags, manual errors, and compliance headaches as highlighted in BDO’s 2026 FinTech predictions.

Solution

LedgerLink listens to payment webhooks, auto-maps tokens to invoices, flags mismatches, and triggers auto-responses for refunds or escalations.

Product & Tech

Connect Stripe/Adyen/PayPal, display a delta dashboard, provide compliance-ready audit logs referencing Visa and BDO data, and ship actionable remediation bots.

Python webhook listeners + Postgres metadata, React dashboard, run on Docker Compose or Fargate for <$100/mo prototypes.
